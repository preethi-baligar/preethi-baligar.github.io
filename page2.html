B. PhD Topic

Title: Instructional design for design problem-solving using cooperative learning in first-year undergraduate engineering.
Purpose Statement: The study developed an instructional model for team-based design problem-solving by integrating cooperative learning with the engineering design process, examining its effect on team effectiveness, performance, satisfaction, and individual learning.
